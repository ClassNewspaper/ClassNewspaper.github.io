Ira Tiwary

 

As you know, we have been on the Oregon Trail for 46 days, and we are right now in the middle of current day Nebraska.  Our journey had been uneventful but one frightful day, a thunderstorm occurs! 

 

Lightning flashes, clouds rumble, and rain pounds down. The dirt trail turns sloshy, and one wagon turns over! The poor Websters lose all their food!  

 

Luckily, we are only nine days away from the nearest trading point, Fort Laramie. The Websters have a decent amount of money left so they can buy more food. Until we reach Fort Laramie, each family will have to share some food with the Websters. 

 

As if that wasn’t bad enough, 3 days away from Fort Laramie, a disease broke out on our wagon train! The Smiths, Millers, and Lucy Fletcher caught a mystery illness. The Millers died! To save the Smiths and Ms. Lucy, we used clues and found out the secret disease to get the antidote. 

 

It was Cholera! Cholera was quite common in the 1840’s. It can be caused by contaminated food and water. Its symptoms are high fever, vomiting, thirst, and diarrhea. Its cure is rehydrated.

Book Suggestions

 

Berenice Lui

On April 9, I put a question in the chat asking what book suggestions you guys have. I will be going over 5 books suggested by order of the time it was posted.  

1.    Yabin suggested the series Steve the Noob! According to google, Steve the Noob is a 46 book nonofficial Minecraft series. The author is Steve, no full name is given. 

2.    Anushka said the series Boxcar Children! The Boxcar Children series has over 166 books and comes out with more every year. Those books are aimed for grades 2-6. The author of this series is Gertrude Chandler Warner  

 

3. Yabin also suggested all books in the series Gamenight999  written by Mark Cheverton. It is in fact another nonofficial Minecraft based series. This series is three books long. 

 

4. Avatar: The Last Airbender (comic version) was greatly suggested by Angela! Avatar: The Last Airbender is written by Guen Luen Yang. The movie, Avatar: The last Airbender was made after the book published, December 2012. 

 

5. Ira said Three Times Lucky! Three Times Lucky is a fiction novel written by Sheila Turnage. It won best seller in 2013 and was Newberry book hounaer in 2013
